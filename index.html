About PhrenTech

Our Mission & Vision

Building the future of accessible, effective mental healthcare through immersive technology.

Our Story

PhrenTech was founded in 2023 by a team of clinical psychologists, VR engineers, and AI researchers who saw an opportunity to revolutionize mental healthcare. Witnessing the growing mental health crisis and the barriers preventing people from accessing quality care, we set out to create a platform that would make therapy more accessible, engaging, and effective.

Today, PhrenTech serves thousands of patients and partners with hundreds of licensed therapists and healthcare organizations worldwide. Our platform has facilitated over 100,000 therapy sessions and continues to expand the boundaries of what's possible in digital mental healthcare.
